Overview

Purpose

The objective is to navigate downstream rivers rapids using an inflated raft. It's considered an extreme sport which is highly challenging and risky and requires a lot of technical skills and teamwork. The races are conducted on river courses. Usually, sprint and slalom events are conducted for rafting.

Adrenaline rushes allow rafters to focus only on the excitement they feel rather than the physical exercise of rafting. Reduces stress – Rafting provides you an escape from reality. The quietness of nature lets you relax and leave all your worries behind you, especially when the river is slow-moving.

Audience

Power of the moving water is incredible. Mastering the rapids will definitely get your heart pumping but it will also fill you with filling of joy and triumph. It is important to choose the right rafting trip according your fitness and experience.

Rafting is a highly dynamic activity that is always unique. When people hear about whitewater rafting they mostly imagine powerful rapids, dangerous environment and adrenaline rush. Rafting can most certainly be all of that, but it can also be much more!
